# Andreas ('Andras') Loeffler

This repository collects public academic profile material for Andreas Loeffler, Professor of Banking and Finance at Freie Universität Berlin.

The profile contains a curriculum vitae, publication lists, bibliographic data, and notes on court citations where they can be verified from reliable sources.

## Contents

- [Curriculum vitae](cv/)
- [Publications](publications/)
- [Court citations](publications/court-citations.md)
- [Archived PDFs](pdfs/)
- [Structured publication data](data/publications.yml)

## Research Areas

- firm valuation (discounted cash flow)
- taxation and valuation
- capital market theory
- decision theory
- statistical software support for the Wilcoxon distribution in R
